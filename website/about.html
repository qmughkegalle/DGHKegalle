About Us

District General Kegalle Hospital is dedicated to providing compassionate, high-quality healthcare to the people of Kegalle and surrounding regions. With a wide range of medical services, modern facilities, and a team of skilled professionals, we strive to ensure that every patient receives the best possible care in a safe and supportive environment.
